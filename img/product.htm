Description

Hyper Black lashes, ultra-defined. Wonder Lash Ultra is a high tech, 5-in-1 multi-benefit mascara that merges a high definition look with superior lash lift – no smudges, no flaking.

Innovative Ultra HD Technology coats each individual lash from the root using the exclusive HD Paddle Brush, delivering long-wearing, instant definition. A breakthrough Digital Pigment Blend makes lashes look intensely black on-screen, with the mascara boasting 1st to the world smartphone colour intensity testing.

Guaranteed to give you a high-definition eyelash look, it’s the ultimate everyday mascara that performs online as good as IRL. Like a HD filter for your lashes. Ophthalmologist tested.

How To Use
How to best apply mascara for an Ultra HD Lash Look

Coat, curl and define each lash instantly – with greater lift and separation from the very root of your lashes – with the HD Paddle Brush.


  1. Using the bristle side of the HD Paddle Brush, start application at the root of the top lashes and slightly wiggle to push the brush into the lashline – this innovative one-sided design allows you to get ultra-close to the lash root for a HD look.
  2. Slowly brush the mascara through lashes, gently pushing upwards and holding halfway for superior lash lift, before coating each lash to the tip
  3. Use brush tip to target extremely small, hard-to-reach inner and outer lashes, holding the brush vertically to load more bulk if required.
  4. Build desired intensity by repeating steps above, before coating bottom lashes using the same technique.

PRO TIP! Use a rotating motion of the brush as you apply the mascara from lash root to tips – this twirl technique allows greater access to over 200 silicone bristles for ultra-high definition.
